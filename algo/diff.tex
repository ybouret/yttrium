Chemical potential:
\begin{equation}
	\mu_i = RT \ln a_i + z_i \mathcal{F} V = RT \ln (\gamma_i[C_i]) + z_i \mathcal{F} V
\end{equation}

Poisson
\begin{equation}
	\Delta V = - \dfrac{\rho}{\epsilon} = -\mathcal{N}_A 
\end{equation}

Molar force:
\begin{equation}
	\vec{F}_i = - \vec{\nabla} \mu_i
\end{equation}
