Around $\vec{M}_0$ and $\vec{T}_0$:
\begin{equation}
	\vec{M}(s) = \vec{M}_0 + \int_0^s 
	\begin{bmatrix}
	\cos \theta(u)\\
	\sin \theta(u)
	\end{bmatrix}
	\, \mathrm{d}u
\end{equation}

We assume:
\begin{equation}
	\theta(u) = \theta_0 + \gamma u 
\end{equation}

\begin{equation}
	\int_0^s \cos (\theta_0 + \gamma u) \, \mathrm{d}u =
	\dfrac{1}{\gamma}\left[ \sin(\theta_0 + \gamma s) - \sin(\theta_0) \right] 
\end{equation}
