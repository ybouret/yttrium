Let $\vec{e}$ be a base vector. The orthogonal of $\vec a$ on $\vec e$ is:
\begin{equation}
	\vec a ^\perp = \vec a - \dfrac{\vec a \cdot \vec e}{\vec e^2} \vec e \implies \vec a ^\perp \cdot \vec e = 0
\end{equation}
